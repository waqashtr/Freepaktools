How to Use This Tool

  1. Upload your image: Click on the upload area or drag and drop your image file into the designated area. The tool supports JPG, PNG, and WEBP formats with a maximum file size of 10MB.
  2. Select upscale factor: Use the slider to choose how much you want to upscale your image (2x, 3x, or 4x the original size).
  3. Choose enhancement mode: Select between Standard (balanced), Sharp Details (for clearer edges), or Smooth (for softer images) depending on your needs.
  4. Process the image: Click the "Upscale Image" button to begin the enhancement process. This may take a few moments depending on your image size.
  5. View and download: Compare the original and upscaled images side by side. If satisfied with the result, click "Download Upscaled Image" to save it to your device.

Tip: For best results, start with the highest quality image available. The upscaler works best with images that are slightly blurry or low-resolution rather than extremely pixelated images.
