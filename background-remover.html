How to Use This Tool

  1. Upload your image: Click on the upload area or drag and drop your image file into the designated area. The tool supports JPG, PNG, and WEBP formats with a maximum file size of 10MB.
  2. Select removal mode: Choose between automatic AI background removal (works best for clear subjects) or manual selection (for more control).
  3. For manual mode: Use the brush or lasso tool to mark areas to keep or remove. Adjust brush size as needed for precise selection.
  4. Process the image: Click the "Remove Background" button to begin the process. For automatic mode, this may take a few moments.
  5. View and download: Compare the original and processed images side by side. Download as PNG for transparent background or JPG with white background.

Tip: For best results with automatic removal, use images with clear contrast between subject and background. Complex backgrounds may require manual touch-up.
