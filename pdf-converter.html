How to Use This Tool

  1. Upload your PDF: Click on the upload area or drag and drop your PDF file into the designated area. The tool supports PDF files with a maximum size of 20MB.
  2. Select output format: Choose the format you want to convert your PDF to from the dropdown menu. Options include Word (DOCX), Excel (XLSX), PowerPoint (PPTX), JPG, PNG, and plain text (TXT).
  3. Adjust conversion options (if available): Depending on the selected format, additional options may appear to customize your conversion.
  4. Convert the file: Click the "Convert PDF" button to begin the conversion process. This may take a few moments depending on your file size and selected format.
  5. Download your file: Once the conversion is complete, click "Download Converted File" to save the converted file to your device.

Note: For complex PDFs with multiple pages or advanced formatting, some elements may not convert perfectly. Always review the converted file to ensure it meets your needs.
